Japanese Cuisine

Sushi

Sushi is made with vinegared rice, often served with raw fish, seaweed, and vegetables.

Ramen

Ramen is a noodle soup dish with rich broth, toppings like egg, pork, and green onions.

Etiquette

Slurping noodles is polite! Say Itadakimasu before eating and Gochisousama after.
